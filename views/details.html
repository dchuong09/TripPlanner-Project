What to explore

Photograph the Golden Gate Bridge, one of the world's most beloved and scenic icons, with a veteran travel photographer. Discover little known perspectives on our scenic hike on the lovely coastal landscape of the Golden Gate. In spring, wildflowers abound on our route and marine mammals and raptors provide a wildlife show. The moody Golden Gate Bridge is always a delight to photograph, sun or fog.
